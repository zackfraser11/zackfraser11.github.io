Final Project

For the final project my partner and I decided to make a game called 21. You haver three turns to get as close to 21 as you can.
If you are closer to 21 than the dealer without going over then you win. If you do go over 21, you lose. Choose a card on the left or right to
add the corresponding points to your score. After three turns the game will decide who wins.
Ace is equal to 1 and face cards are equal to 10.
